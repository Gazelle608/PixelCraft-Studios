Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does a typical design project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A logo design typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a complete website design can take 6-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline at the start of each project.

What is your payment structure?

+

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you offer revisions?

+

Yes! We include up to 3 rounds of revisions for all design concepts to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

What file formats do you provide?

+

We provide all necessary file formats for your project. For logos, this includes vector files (AI, EPS, SVG), high-resolution PNGs, and JPGs. For websites, we provide all source files and documentation.

Do you work with international clients?

+

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and can accommodate different time zones through scheduled virtual meetings and efficient communication tools.
